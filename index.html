My Trip to Paris

1 Last summer, I visited Paris, 2 a beautiful city in France. 3 I arrived at the train station early in the morning. 4 My hotel was near the Eiffel Tower, 5 so I asked for directions.

6 The man said, “Go straight, then turn left at the corner. 7 You will see a roundabout. 8 Take the first exit and go past the bakery. 9 The hotel is next to the bank and opposite the café.”

10 I followed his directions. 11 I walked across the street and went past a small park. 12 I saw the roundabout and took the first exit. 13 The bakery was on my right, and the bank was beside it. 14 My hotel was in front of the bank and behind the café. 15 I was very happy to find it!

16 That afternoon, I visited the Eiffel Tower. 17 It was so tall! I stood below it and looked above to see the top. 18 I took the elevator up and saw all of Paris beneath me. 19 The view was amazing!

20 Later, I went to a museum by the river. 21 It was between two big buildings. 22 I saw beautiful paintings in the museum. 23 Some people were sitting under the trees in front of the building, enjoying the sun.

24 After the museum, I ate dinner at a restaurant close to my hotel. 25 It was among many other small shops. 26 I sat at a table next to the window and watched people walk across the street.
